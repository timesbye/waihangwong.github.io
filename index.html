About

Academic Profile

I am currently an undergraduate student in Artificial Intelligence at Wuhan University. My work focuses on building and evaluating intelligent agents that can reason over trajectories, handle constraints, and interact with humans more effectively.

I am also an incoming graduate student in Artificial Intelligence at Tsinghua University. I am especially interested in LLM-based agents, embodied AI, world models, human-AI interaction, and evaluation methods for interactive decision-making systems.

Research

Research Interests

LLM Agents

Reasoning, planning, tool use, and interaction in language-model-based agents.

Embodied AI

Interactive agents that perceive, act, and communicate in virtual or physical environments.

World Models

Representations and predictive models for decision-making and long-horizon behavior.

Human-AI Interaction

Clarification, uncertainty expression, and user-centered interactive systems.

Evaluation protocols for constraint following, task completion, and interactive decision-making.

Selected Projects

OpenAvatar-AgentUI

  • Embodied AI
  • Virtual Agent
  • Unity

A Unity-based virtual avatar agent interface that integrates dialogue state, emotion, action control, and multimodal interaction. The system explores how virtual agents can express reasoning states and interact with users through speech, facial expression, and body actions.

  • Dialogue state and emotion visualization

  • Multimodal human-agent interaction
